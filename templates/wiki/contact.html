Contacts


Prior to creating an event, it is important to create at least one contact. This contact person will be the point of contact for shipping information for the event.

Adding a new Contact


To add a new contact to the system:

  • Click Tables and then click Contacts.

  • Click Add Contact button in the left corner.

  • Fill out Basic Information page, which includes: First Name, Last Name, phone number, email, and company they work for. NOTE: fields marked with asterisk cannot be left blank.

  • Click on Shipping Address tab and enter the shipping information for this contact.

  • Click Save
